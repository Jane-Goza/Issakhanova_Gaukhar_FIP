About us

BlueSteel sports is the result of our creative experimentation in the art of Australian tea tree beverage creation, born through many iterations. Beer meets with an intense aroma, in which notes of coriander and orange are easily guessed.

The palate is balanced and dry, while the finish is long with a noticeable amount of sweetness - although not sugary.

The presence of tea tree makes the aroma fresh, and gives the taste a subtle cooling effect, making acquaintance with this drink more interesting.
